Roles

  • Member of Student Event Center (later, Campus Events + Entertainment) Asian American Culture Committee from 2011 to 2015, and Event Coordinator for the 2012-2013 academic year.

    Responsibilities as Event Coordinator included:
    • Locating and securing venues
    • Coordinating weekly meetings with co-chairs
    • Contacting local vendors and guest speakers
    • Tracking and allocating budget
    • Deciding purchases for events with advisor
    • Verifying posters and flyers for accuracy and content
    • Creating and advertising event page via Facebook
    • Consulting with UTPD for event security
    • Participating in and overseeing setup, event activities, and breakdown
    • Gathering, reviewing, and writing down feedback and suggestions

  • Member of Campus Events + Entertainment Recreation from 2013 to 2015, and Membership Development officer for the 2013-2015 academic year.

    Responsibilities as Membership Development officer included:
    • Advertising and recruiting for the committee
    • Securing membership retention
    • Coming up with weekly icebreakers
    • Keeping track of weekly attendance during meeting
    • Sending out meeting recap emails
    • Setting up and overseeing group socials
    • Being part of all event coordinating process

Awards

  • Member of the Year for Student Events Center Asian American Culture Committee, awarded in April 2013.
  • Member of the Year for Campus Events + Entertainment Recreation, awarded in April 2014.
  • Campus Events + Entertainment "Take Action" STAR Award, awarded in April 2014.
    This person is a particularly spirited volunteer, and relishes every opportunity to help out. They exhibit this eagerness for all of E+E events, regardless of who is involved. Wherever help is needed, they can be counted on to answer the call.
