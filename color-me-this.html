Color Me This! is a simple camera app created to help users easily determine colors they are unsure about. It mainly focuses on helping the color blind determine specific colors or making graphic designers' lives easier by providing them the information they need to get their favorite or needed colors.

With Color Me This! you can take a photo or upload an image from your gallery and find the closest color associated to a selected pixel. You can check the name, hexadecimal value, and RGB values of that color. You can also save your favorite colors in your Palette!

Disclaimer: Depending on the lighting of a photo or your camera quality, the color values returned may not be exactly true-to-life.

Features

  • A camera preview containing an easy-to-locate overflow menu, a clean shutter button, and an upload-from-gallery button with your latest photo as the image!
  • The ability to show or hide the color names, hexadecimal values, and RGB values!
  • A full-screen immersive workspace for maximum visibility with options to go back to the camera or to upload another image!
  • The ability to copy the hexadecimal value of a color from the workspace's button or from My Palette!
  • The ability to save colors to My Palette, containing your favorite colors and their name, hexadecimal value, and RGB values!.

Future Updates

  • The ability to use both the front and back camera.
  • The ability to turn on / off / auto flash.
  • The ability to add filters to brighten / darken the photo for additional accuracy.
